    Overview

    NeuroSpeak is an advanced speech recognition and synthesis platform powered by neural networks. It enables natural language processing with high accuracy and low latency, making it ideal for accessibility applications, voice assistants, and multilingual communication tools.

      Instant speech-to-text with minimal latency

    • Multilingual Support

      Recognition and synthesis in multiple languages

    • Speaker Identification

    • Natural Voice Synthesis

      Human-like speech generation with emotion

    • Context Awareness

      Understanding conversational context and references

    • Accessibility Tools

      Features designed for users with disabilities
